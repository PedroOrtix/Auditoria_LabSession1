Who we are

AIDA is a multidisciplinary research group located at the Technical University of Madrid. Our main research areas are: Machine Learning (Classification, Clustering, Hidden Markov Models), Decision Support Systems, Computational Intelligence, Evolutionary Computation, Data Mining & Social Nerwork Analysis, Big Data, and Intelligent Systems for Industry. With more than 300 publications and 40 research and industrial projects, the group has an outstanding experience in both research cooperation and partnership consortium.
