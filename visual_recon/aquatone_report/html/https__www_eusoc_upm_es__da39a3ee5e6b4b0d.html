Who are we

E-USOC (Spanish User Support and Operations Centre) is a centre of Polytechnic University of Madrid (UPM) specialized in Research and Development (R&D) activities in the fields of space science and technology.

What do we do

E-USOC is the point of contact for the Spanish user teams developing experiments which require microgravity environment, such as the International Space Station, Space Shuttle missions, Parabolic Flights and so on.

Where are we

E-USOC is located at Campus de Montegancedo, a technology Campus of the Universidad Politcnica de Madrid (UPM), which was awarded as Campus of International Excellence in 2010. The Campus is located on the outskirts of Madrid, in the town of Pozuelo de Alarcn.
