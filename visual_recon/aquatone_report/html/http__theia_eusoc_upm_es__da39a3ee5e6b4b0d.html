Theia Space is an initiative dedicated to providing innovative solutions in the field of space systems engineering. Our activities range from the development of nanosatellite missions and satellite subsystems to the design of innovative attitude and orbit control algorithms, currently being tested on board nanosatellite missions.
Our main commercial success lies in the field of educational nanosatellites. We are the developers of ESAT, a hands-on training satellite used by many universities and institutions worldwide to teach real satellite integration and operations. The ESA Academy has also selected the ESAT for several of its training programmes over the past years.
