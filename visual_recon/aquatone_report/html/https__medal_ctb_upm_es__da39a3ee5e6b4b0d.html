WELCOME TO

MEDICAL DATA ANALYTICS LABORATORY

MEDAL (Medical Data Analytics Laboratory) is a laboratory that belongs to the Center for Biomedical Technology (CTB) at Universidad Politécnica de Madrid (UPM). MEDAL is composed of a subset of both professors and researchers from the UPM research group MIDAS (Data Mining and Simulation). MEDAL research lines are focused on the extraction, analysis, integration, and interpretation of health data in its diverse forms (images, text, structured data, ….). Our team is involved in various national and international projects and composed of different professors, researchers, students, and management staff.
